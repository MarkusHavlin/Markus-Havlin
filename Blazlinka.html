Doritos

Goldfish Cheetos Popcorners Table
Doritos are another fantastic snack, a staple of the industry. It is nearly impossible to meet anyone who has not tried them before. But what makes them so good? In my opinion and also in a lot of others it comes down to 3 main things, shape, the shape is iconic and helps with the texture of it especially with the crunch. Another is flavor, the taste of the two main Dorito types is a staple of just snacks. Finally it is most definitely the dust, the dust that remains is very good because it has been proven that dopamine levels go up whilst sucking off the dust from the chips.

Fun facts: Nacho Doritos are nearly 50 years old! (1974) Frito-Lay spent $50 million—the most in the country’s history—to revamp their most popular product, according to The New York Times. The overhaul included market research with chip eaters and grocery stores. The result? Doritos that have rounded corners, more seasoning and were 15% thinner and 20% longer. Finally, Doritos actually make VERY good firestarters.

Arch West reportedly came up with the idea of Doritos when he was on vacation with his family in Mexico

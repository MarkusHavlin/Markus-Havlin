Cheetos

Goldfish Doritos Popcorners Table
Cheetos as a whole are one of my favorite snacks mainly because of one of their spin-offs the Flamin Hot Cheetos are a wonderful snack, they have Just the right amount of heat not to mention the chilie poweder taste great I also like couple of their other spin offs like the puffs. I am also very impressed with how good the texture the crunchy cheetos are.

Fun facts: The person who invented the "Flamin Hot" Cheeto (the most sold type) worked originally as a janitor and once the machine stopped producing the cheese dust he swapped it out for a homemade flavor. That flavor is what started the Flamin' Hot story. Another is the fact that their mascot throughout the years has probably gone under the least amount of changes compared to most other companies. Finally, other countries offer different flavors that what are in others, kinda like the Kitkat.

Charles Elmer Doolin is the founder of the Frito company and also is the person who invented the original Cheeto.

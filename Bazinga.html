Goldfish

Goldfish are some of the best snacks out there for multiple reasons. The main reason I believe that is mainly because of how they balance being salty and they really arent insanely cheesy making the eating expirience very pleasent and enjoyable, not to mention the simple fact that they pair well with a lot of sauces, the one that comes to mind for me at least is ranch.

Fun facts: 40% of all goldfish (since 1997) have the iconic smile, Willard Ohio produces the largest amount of goldfish globally producing around 50 million goldfish a day! Finally, the reason goldfish are fish shaped is because of the creator's astrology sign is a Pisces which is usually represented by a fish

Margaret Rudkin is the founder of the Pepperidgefarm and also is the person who ported the goldfish from Switzerland. She found the Goldfish crackers whilst she was on a tour of the country.

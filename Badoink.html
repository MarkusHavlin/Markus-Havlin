Popcorners

Goldfish Cheetos Doritos Table
Popcorners are relatively new to the snack game, however they have definitely made their presence know, as they are now one of the best snacks around. Whilst these snacks have a similar texture to rice cakes, flavor-wise it couldn't be farther from the truth. THese snacks are amazing with their wide arrange of flavors and nice balance of chip-to-flavor ratio, making them an ideal snack.

Fun facts: Popcorners are a lot healthier snack than others coming in at about the sodium calories etc. Another is that they are only air-popped and not fried. The last one is that they have one of the most popular Super Bowl ads in recent times, using the iconic show "Breaking Bad" and making a spin-off of it.
